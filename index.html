The Recipe Process

  1. We start with roasting the beef
  2. we chop up cabbage in small pieces
  3. then when the beef is ready we place it on the burger buns in a bowl
  4. then we add half of cabbage in between the buns
  5. then we a bit of ketchup and sour cream
  6. then we add the rest of the cabbage between the buns and a little more sauce
  7. then you mix the salad with the sauce
